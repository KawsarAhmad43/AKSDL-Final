About AKSD

We are a creative and innovative structural & design company, solving problems and shaping the world around us. High-quality designs and innovative solutions can only be achieved by structural engineers who bring creativity and practicality to the design process.

With the aim of bold investment, technical innovation and strict quality management, A.K Structural & Designer’s Ltd is one of the leading professional structural company in Bangladesh, Since the establishment in 2006. AKSD has been investing in Real Estate and establishing the fabrication shop in Bangladesh and overseas. By training exceptional skillful human resources, organizing the self-design team and implementing ERP for management system.

Within a short span of 14 years, AKSD has developed various numbers of projects in Dhaka; ranging from residential, commercial to land development and hence earned a high level of respect & trust in Bangladesh market. Quality, Commitment, Trust and Customer satisfaction are the ultimate basis to achieve success of A.K Structural & Designer Ltd by creating ‘Your Prestigious Living’, in Bangladesh.

AKSD now lead a diverse, multifaceted, dynamic, enthusiastic and creative group of structural engineers and designers from a wide pool of structural, steelwork fabrication and manufacturing sectors, working together to customize a your dream home.
